Market Growth & Future Projections

The Indian eHealth market is poised for significant growth, driven by strong government initiatives and increasing consumer adoption. This section provides a data-backed outlook on the market size and highlights key catalysts that will fuel the expansion of platforms like Fidicure.

Projected Growth of India's Digital Health Market (USD Billion)

The market is expected to grow significantly, supported by policy and consumer trends. Source: NITI Aayog Reports & IBEF Compilations.

Catalyst 1: Government Policy

The Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission (ABDM) aims to create a national digital health ecosystem with unique health IDs and interoperable data standards. Platforms like Fidicure can integrate with this infrastructure, enhancing their credibility and utility for millions of users.

Catalyst 2: Consumer Behaviour

Studies from institutions like IIM-Ahmedabad show a marked increase in consumer willingness to use digital platforms for healthcare needs post-pandemic. This behavioral shift towards convenience and online services is a powerful tailwind for growth.
